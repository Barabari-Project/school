Commands:

The grid points are just to help you! Feel free to think outside the box :). Purple Hexy will follow you wherever you go

M x,y Move pen to x,y
L x,y Draw line to x,y
h dx Draw horizontal line to x+dx
v dy Draw vertical line to y+dy
l dx,dy Draw line by x+dx,y+dy
